Volumina and Concentration Calculator

Created by Darius Zarse for a uni project at IUBH.


This tool is designed to help with calculating molarity and concentration of solutions mixed in chemical biology.
Get started by:
1. Entering the molarity of your solutions into the "available molarity" field(s).
2. Entering the desired molarity of the solution you want to create and select the correct unit.
3. Entering the amount of solution you want to create.
4. Make sure to have the right unit selected (M, mM, µM, ml, µl) and press the "Calculate" button.
