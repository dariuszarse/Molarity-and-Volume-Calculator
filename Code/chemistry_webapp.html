Volumina and Concentration Calculator

Created by Darius Zarse for a uni project at IUBH.


This tool is designed to help with calculating molarity and concentration of solutions mixed in chemical biology, but feel free to use it any way that is useful to you.
Simply get started by entering the molarity of your solutions into the "Concentration of available solution" field(s). Make sure to have the right unit selected (M, mM, µM).
Next, enter the desired molarity of the solution you want to create and select the correct unit.
Finally, enter the amount of solution you want to create and press the "Calculate" button.
Enjoy!
